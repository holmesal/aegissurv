This security camera is on the cutting edge of technology by utilizing the Sprint cellular network along with advanced motion activated features on the camera itself, to provide for a prime-time security system. The camera is completely motion activated and takes pictures whenever motion in front of it is detected. These pictures are then immediately uploaded to a secure database to where only an authorized user may access them and configure camera settings. More advanced features include notification to the owner via email if the camera detects motion at a pre-set time. For example, lets say that you know for a fact no one is going to be, or should be, in your backyard between the hours of 11pm and 4am. You simply set these times on our website in your account, and if motion is detected and a picture is taken, you are immediately notified via email. However, an email is only sent out during theses pre-set times. We assumed that you'd find it annoying to get an email every time the camera detected motion throughout the day.
