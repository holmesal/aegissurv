Simple, universal surveillance. Only $249

This security camera is on the cutting edge of technology by utilizing the Sprint cellular network along with advanced motion activated features on the camera itself, to provide for a prime-time security system. The camera is completely motion activated and takes pictures whenever motion in front of it is detected. These pictures are then immediately uploaded to a secure database to where only an authorized user may access them and configure camera settings. More advanced features include notification to the owner via email if the camera detects motion at a pre-set time. For example, lets say that you know for a fact no one is going to be, or should be, in your backyard between the hours of 11pm and 4am. You simply set these times on our website in your account, and if motion is detected and a picture is taken, you are immediately notified via email. However, an email is only sent out during theses pre-set times. We assumed that you'd find it annoying to get an email every time the camera detected motion throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How difficult is it to set up the cellular system?

Not difficult at all! It is literally plug and play. As soon as the camera system has power, it starts transmitting. However, in order to link the camera to your account, you'll have to type in the camera ID # and serial # in order to view the pictures and configure settings.

What do you mean by 'configure settings'?

I mean additional features that can be configured. For example, you can have the camera set to send you an email alert if the motion activation goes off between certain set times.

How much does it cost per month?

The monthly fee is a low $40 and no contract is required.

Do I have to pay extra for additional features such as email alerts?

No, all those extra features are included.

What is the warrenty policy and the return policy?

We have a 50 day return policy and a 1-year warrenty. Basically if the product breaks within one-year, we will fix it for free however you only have 50 days for a full refund if you aren't completely satisfied with the product.

Why is the solar module significantly more expensive than the regular plug-in module?

Solar panels are expensive to begin with, even for manufacturer like us. Also, the solar module contains a lithium battery pack built in so that the unit can continue to operate throughout the night. These two items unforutately significantly increase the cost of the unit.

Where is the camera system made?

The cameras are assembled right here in the US with US labor. Only some parts are made oversees and this is simply because they are not made in the US, not because they are cheaper and we are greedy and hence we're trying to increase our profit margin.

When do you ship to?

Aegis Surveillance on offers support and only ships within the 48 continental United States. This is due to our policy with the Sprint network in that the cameras only work in areas covered by Sprint 3G or 4G. For a map of the Sprint network, please click here.

Is there a lower cost version?

We are expanding our product line and are preparing to launch a camera module that uses a SIM card instead of the cellular network. This is a lower cost alternative with no monthly fees. Currently the SIM card can store data for up to 4 months however an option of 1 year will be available. The SIM card simply sticks into the backs and pictures are recorded whenever there is motion. To review the data, simply pull out the SIM card and stick it into your Windows/Mac computer to view all the pictures data for the time period.
